Your Harvest, Our Network.

A partnership rooted in shared values, connecting dedicated growers with those who value exceptional, sustainably-farmed produce.

A Direct Partnership for Your Farm

We believe in fostering strong, transparent relationships that benefit local farmers and nourish our communities.

Expanded Reach, Conscious Consumers

Tap into a curated network of buyers, all seeking the quality and story behind your produce. We connect your produce directly to appreciative customers.

  • Access to diverse, values-aligned wholesale customers.
  • Consistent demand for seasonal, high-quality produce.
  • Your farm's story shared with an engaged audience.

Transparent & Fair Value

Your dedication deserves fair compensation. We operate with transparency in pricing and clear communication, ensuring you understand the journey of your produce and the value it commands. Our partnerships are structured to be mutually beneficial and sustainable for the long term.

We believe getting your produce to market should be straightforward. Our team handles the logistics efficiently, always prioritizing the careful handling required to protect the value and freshness of your work.

Our Straightforward Process

Our onboarding process is designed to be clear and efficient, allowing you to focus on your farming.

01

Share Your Farm Details

Start by filling out our Farmer Inquiry form – you'll find the link at the bottom of this page. This gives us a good initial overview of your farm, what you grow, your farming practices, and your general availability.

02

Let's Talk It Over

Once we've had a chance to review your inquiry, we'll get in touch to schedule a conversation. This is where we’ll discuss your specific harvest schedules, work out fair pricing, plan the best way to get your produce to us, and chat more about your unique approach to farming.

03

Getting Your Produce Moving

With our partnership set, we'll arrange how to best get your produce, via pick-ups or delivery. We then handle connecting your harvest to buyers, managing orders and payments, and ensuring fresh delivery, so you can concentrate on growing.
