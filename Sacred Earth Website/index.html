Who We Are

Sacred Earth Produce is a family-run distributor based in Southern California. We started in 1994 with a pickup truck loaded with fruit from our own grove. Today, we source directly from small farms across the state and move produce to buyers who care about where their food comes from. Our focus is on real relationships, fair sourcing, and keeping the food system local and honest.
